What do I do?

This webpage lets you see the number of games released each year for a given character

  • 1. Enter a character name in the search field and clck the search button.
  • 2. On the left side of the screen search results will appear matching your character name.
  • 3. Click on a character's description to see a graph of their game appearances on the right side.
